AI for Youth 2020

CBSE, in collaboration with Intel, is organizing an ‘AI For Youth’ virtual symposium from October 13-17, 2020. The symposium will enable Youth to learn about AI readiness skills and other emerging technologies from global leaders, gain an immersive experience and witness AI social impact projects created by their own peers.
