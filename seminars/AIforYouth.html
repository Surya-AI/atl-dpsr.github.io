AI for Youth 2020

About

CBSE, in collaboration with Intel, is organizing an ‘AI For Youth’ virtual symposiumfrom October 13 -17, 2020. The symposium will enable Youth to learn about AI readiness skills and other emerging technologiesfrom global leaders, gain an immersive experience and witness AI social impact projects created by their own peers.
This symposium will be open for Students (class 8 and above), Principals and Teachers of all schools pan India. The attendees will be able to access sessions on diverse topics by national and internationalspeakers, visit the showcase zone and interact with presenters, join in the experiential zone and attend live webinars. A detailed agenda is attached as AnnexureI.
In addition, we may also attempt on setting a Guinness World Record for Building AI readiness skills in students during the symposium. More details will be shared post registration.
In this regard, the Heads of all CBSE affiliated schools are requested to circulate this information with students and teachers and encouragethem to participate in the symposium.
Being an online event, it is important to gain consent of parents and guardians. Thus, to complete the registration process and get access to symposium every student must share a consent form (Annexure II) signed by the parent. This form once signed can be uploaded here can be sent to help@aiforyouth.org along with the name and email address of the child to pre-register for the event.
Principals and Teachers can simply send in an e-mail at help@aiforyouth.org sharing their name, designation, school name and email id. In case of larger number of teachers participating from the same school please send in a single e-mail with all details.
For any other details please feel free to call the helpline number +91-9964600800.
