RAMAN Awards 2020

About

Official Website - ramanaward.org
RAMAN Awards is a registered non profit trust created among others for promoting young scientific acumen among the youth of India. We support young creators and ecourage them to publish their findings and explore the various branches of science, learning different scientific theories and laws.

Submission Details

The shortlisting process will require children to develop a “new” hands-on, experiential science activity in any of the shortlisted topics of science. The submission, which is made on the award website, should include the following:

  1. Scientific Principle addressed
  2. A checklist filled by the participant which brings out:
    1. Originality of the idea. If the idea is a variation of an existing idea, then provide link to existing idea
    2. Effectiveness with which it conveys the chosen topic
    3. Ease of access to materials required
    4. Fun – how much will a child enjoy the activity?
  3. Photos, Illustration and / or Video of the activity
  4. A short description of the science behind the activity and the steps performed
  5. Materials Used (and their availability)
Review a sample submission in the Resources section of the website and download it in the Download page.

Eligibility

  1. Proof of Eligibility, Parental Consent. To participate in the Contest:
    • You must be at least eight (8) years of age and no older than sixteen (16) years of age on 1st January 2020 to participate.
    • Your parent or legal guardian must consent to your participation and agree to these Contest Rules (“Parental Consent”).
    • Finalists will have to provide a bona fide certificate from their school or birth certificate in case a child is home schooled.
  2. Requirements to Enter. In order to participate in the Contest, you must have:
    • a valid mobile phone number
    • a valid postal address
    • a valid email address
    • a valid email address
    • access to the Internet is recommended
  3. Verifying Eligibility

    ISPF reserves the right to verify your eligibility and to adjudicate on any dispute at any time. You agree to provide ISPF with any proof of eligibility requested by ISPF and your refusal or failure to timely provide such proof may result in your disqualification from the Contest.

  4. Communications

    All communications between ISPF and you, including the Co5test Website and email communications, will be in English.

How to Register

to enter the Contest, you must first register on the Contest Website during the listed registration times.

How to Submit an Entry Once Registered

To submit an entry to the Contest, you should visit the Contest website and provide the required details.In case you do not have access to the Internet, you may send the details to the address provided on the Contest website to reach before the deadline. Six entries are allowed per contestant at the Preliminary stage and one entry at the National Finals.

Judging

Judging will be done by volunteers and a panel of scientists. ISPF’s decision in this matter is final and binding.

Disqualification

You may be disqualified from the Contest, forfeit any prizes you may be eligible to receive, and prohibited from participating in any future ISPF Contests if ISPF reasonably believes that you have attempted to undermine the legitimate operation of the Contest, including:

  1. Providing false information about yourself during registration or concerning your eligibility
  2. Breaching or refusing to comply with these Contest Rules
  3. Threatening or harassing other contestants, ISPF, Co-sponsors, or any Organisation, including their employees and representatives
  4. Tampering or interfering with administration of the Contest or with the ability of other contestants to participate in the Contest
  5. Submitting content in an entry that:
    • is not original
    • violates the rights of a third part
    • is lewd, obscene, pornographic, racist, sexist, or otherwise inappropriate to the Contest
    • violates any applicable law

Your Representations, Warranties, Indemnities

Accourding to Representations and Warranties. You represent and warrant that:

  1. the information you provide about yourself while registering or in subsequent communications with ISPF is truthful and accurate
  2. your entries are original
  3. you own all rights in your entries
  4. your entries do not violate any applicable laws
  5. your entries do not violate any rights of any other person or entity or any obligation you may have with them.
